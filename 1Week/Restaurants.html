5 Best Restaurants in Rochester, NY

  1. Good Luck

    Good Luck is a stylish restaurant in the heart of Rochester's Neighborhood of the Arts, serving creative small plates, craft cocktails, and an extensive wine list.

  2. Lento

    Lento is a farm-to-table restaurant located in the Village Gate square, offering fresh, seasonal ingredients in its ever-changing menu of American cuisine.

  3. The Owl House

    The Owl House is a vegan restaurant and bar serving creative, delicious dishes and cocktails in a casual, welcoming atmosphere in the heart of Rochester's South Wedge neighborhood.

  4. Voula's Greek Sweets

    Voula's Greek Sweets is a bakery and cafe specializing in authentic Greek desserts and coffee, as well as savory Greek dishes, located in the heart of Rochester's East End.

  5. The Red Fern

    The Red Fern is a vegan cafe offering tasty, healthy, and sustainable food and drink options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner in Rochester's trendy Park Avenue neighborhood.
